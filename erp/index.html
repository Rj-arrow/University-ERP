Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM) is a multi-sector, private university located in Odisha, India. Accredited with NAAC with ‘A’ grade, the main campus of CUTM is located at Paralakhemundi (Gajapati district). Centurion University has other campuses located in Bhubaneswar and Visakhapatnam (or Vizianagaram) along with three regional campuses located in Rayagarh, Balangir (or Bolangir) and Balasore.
